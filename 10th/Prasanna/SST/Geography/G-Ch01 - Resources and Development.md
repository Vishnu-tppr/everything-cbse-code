# G-Ch01 — Resources and Development

> **Geography** | **Marks**: Part of 20 | [[🌍 Social Science]]

## Types of Resources

| Basis | Types |
|-------|-------|
| Origin | Biotic (living), Abiotic (non-living) |
| Exhaustibility | Renewable, Non-renewable |
| Ownership | Individual, Community, National, International |
| Development | Potential, Developed, Stock, Reserve |

## Soil Types in India

| Soil | Color | Found In | Crops |
|------|-------|----------|-------|
| Alluvial | Light | Indo-Gangetic plain, deltas | Rice, wheat, sugarcane |
| Black (Regur) | Black | DECBan (Maharashtra, Gujarat) | Cotton |
| Red & Yellow | Red | Odisha, Chhattisgarh, S. Plateau | Millets |
| Laterite | Reddish | Western Ghats, NE | Tea, coffee, cashew |
| Arid | Sandy | Rajasthan, Gujarat | Drought-resistant crops |
| Forest & Mountain | Loamy | Hilly regions | Orchards |

## Soil Conservation Methods

- Contour ploughing, terrace farming, shelter belts
- Strip cropping, crop rotation
- Afforestation

## Common Exam Traps ⚠️

1. Not differentiating Potential vs Developed resources
2. Confusing soil types (Black = cotton, NOT wheat)
3. Map: locating soil types incorrectly
